5 Tips for Glowing Skin

Maintaining healthy hair starts with simple routines. First, use sulfate-free shampoos to avoid stripping natural oils. Second, incorporate deep conditioning weekly with masks rich in keratin. Third, trim split ends every 6-8 weeks to prevent further damage. Fourth, protect hair from heat by using thermal sprays before styling. Finally, eat a balanced diet with biotin-rich foods like eggs and nuts for internal nourishment. These tips can transform your hair's texture and shine.
